## 📸 Recommended Demo Video Flow (Screen Record)
*Recruiters love 30-60 second videos. Don't just post screenshots.*

1.  **The Hook (0:00-0:05):** Start on the **Home Page**. Scroll down slowly to show the "Hero Section", "Featured Categories", and "Product Grid".
2.  **The Interaction (0:05-0:15):**
    *   Hover over a product.
    *   Click **"Quick View"** (shows the modal).
    *   Close it and click a **"Category"** (e.g., Gaming) to show the smooth scroll & filter effect.
3.  **The "Tech Flex" (0:15-0:25):**
    *   Click **"Status"** in the nav.
    *   Show off the **Mission Control Dashboard** (Latency charts, Architecture nodes). This proves you understand monitoring.
4.  **The User Journey (0:25-0:35):**
    *   Click **"Orders"**.
    *   Show the **Order History** with the "Tracking Stepper" (Placed -> Delivered).
5.  **The Architecture (Optional):** Flash a diagram of your Microservices (Spring Boot, Kafka, Eureka).
